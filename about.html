About Us

Hasnuri Sdn. Bhd. was established on 2014, offers a modern alternative for modest clothing to meet the growing demand for Islamic fashion. The brand aims to be a true representation of Islamic attire, emphasizing modesty, Syariah compliance, and versatile designs suitable for various occasions. HASNURI.COM takes pride in delivering exclusive, high-quality designs at affordable prices, ensuring style and accessibility for its customers.

VISION

Hasnuri become the largest manufacturer of 'Baju Kurung' in Malaysia. Helping over 1000 Hasnuri entrepreneurs reach the first 1 million by 2030.

MISSION

Helping women to make Baju Kurung as daily wear, looking beautiful, polite at all times in addition to being able to cover the aurah perfectly.
